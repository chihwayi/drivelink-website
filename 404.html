About DriveLink

Connecting MarketsWorldwide

With over 15 years of experience in automotive import and export, DriveLink has become the trusted partner for businesses and individuals looking to navigate the global vehicle market.

Founded on Excellence

DriveLink was born from a simple vision: to make vehicle import and export accessible, transparent, and reliable for everyone. What started as a small family business has grown into a global network spanning over 50 countries.

Our journey began when our founder, Michael Johnson, experienced firsthand the challenges of importing a vehicle from overseas. The complex paperwork, hidden fees, and lack of transparency motivated him to create a better solution.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does vehicle import typically take?

Vehicle import process usually takes 6-8 weeks from order confirmation to delivery, depending on the origin country and vehicle specifications.

Do you provide financing options?

Yes, we work with multiple financial institutions to provide competitive financing options. Our team can help you find the best rates and terms.

What documentation do I need for vehicle export?

Required documents include vehicle registration, proof of ownership, export permit, and compliance certificates. We handle most paperwork for you.

Can I track my vehicle shipment?

Absolutely! We provide real-time tracking information and regular updates throughout the shipping process via our customer portal.

Do you offer warranty on imported vehicles?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranty coverage on all our imported vehicles, with options for extended warranty packages.
