Frequently AskedQuestionson Vehicle Exports from South Africa

Find answers to the most common questions about our vehicle export services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team for personalized assistance.

Export

Does Goldstar's export services include pre-owned cars?

Yes, Goldstar's export services include pre-owned cars. We handle the export of both new and pre-owned vehicles to various African countries, ensuring all necessary documentation and compliance requirements are met.

Export

Does Goldstar take care of all the paperwork and compliance matters related to vehicle exports?

Yes, at Goldstar we take care of all the paperwork and compliance matters. This includes obtaining export permits, handling registration documents, police clearance certificates, bills of lading, and commercial invoices.

Export

What export services are offered to car dealerships?

We offer comprehensive export services to car dealerships including bulk vehicle exports, documentation handling, logistics coordination, shipping arrangements, and compliance management for multiple vehicle exports.

Export

How do you ensure safe transportation and arrival of vehicles?

We ensure safe transportation through professional shipping partners, secure container loading, comprehensive insurance coverage, real-time tracking, and proper vehicle preparation for international transport.

Export

What costs will I incur when exporting a vehicle?

Export costs include documentation fees, shipping charges, insurance, customs duties, port charges, and destination country import duties. We provide detailed cost breakdowns for complete transparency.

Export

Why should I choose Goldstar?

Choose Goldstar for our extensive experience in African vehicle exports, comprehensive service handling, competitive pricing, reliable shipping partners, and excellent customer support throughout the export process.

General

What documents are required to export a vehicle from South Africa?

You will need the vehicle's registration documents, proof of ownership, an export permit, and a police clearance certificate. Additionally, you may need a bill of lading and a commercial invoice. At Goldstar we take care of all the paperwork.

General

How long does it take to export a vehicle to another African country?

The duration varies based on the destination and shipping method, but it typically takes between 4 to 8 weeks from the date of shipment.

General

Are there any vehicle restrictions for export?

Yes, some countries have restrictions on vehicle age, emissions standards, and left-hand or right-hand drive specifications. It's important to check the regulations of the destination country.

General

How is the vehicle transported?

Vehicles can be transported by sea in a container or via roll-on/roll-off (RoRo) services. The method depends on the destination and the exporter's preference.

General

How do I ensure my vehicle meets the destination country's standards?

Research the specific import regulations of the destination country, including any required modifications to meet safety and emissions standards.

General

What is the process for clearing customs at the destination?

Upon arrival, the vehicle must clear customs. This involves submitting necessary documentation, paying applicable duties and taxes, and passing any required inspections.
