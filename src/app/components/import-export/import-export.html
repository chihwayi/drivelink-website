Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our import and export services

How long does the import process take?

Import times vary by destination country, typically ranging from 2-8 weeks. Our process timeline above shows the detailed breakdown for each step.

What documents are required for importing?

We handle all documentation including export permits, shipping documents, import permits, and customs clearance. You'll need to provide identification and proof of funds.

Can you help with financing?

Yes, we work with financing partners and can assist with payment plans for qualifying customers. Contact us to discuss your financing options.

Do you provide vehicle inspections?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive vehicle inspections and quality assurance before shipping to ensure you receive exactly what you ordered.

What about warranty and after-sales support?

We provide warranty coverage on applicable vehicles and offer ongoing support for parts, service, and maintenance assistance.

Can you source specific vehicle models?

Yes! We specialize in sourcing specific vehicle models, colors, and specifications according to your exact requirements.
