About DriveLink

Connecting Markets Worldwide

With over 5 years of experience in automotive import and export, DriveLink has become the trusted partner for businesses and individuals looking to navigate the global vehicle market.

Founded on Excellence

DriveLink was born from a simple vision: to make vehicle import and export accessible, transparent, and reliable for everyone. What started as a small family business has grown into a global network spanning over 50 countries.

Our journey began when our founder, Michael Johnson, experienced firsthand the challenges of importing a vehicle from overseas. The complex paperwork, hidden fees, and lack of transparency motivated him to create a better solution.
